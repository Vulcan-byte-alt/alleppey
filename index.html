OUR STORY

Kerala's Hidden Paradise

Nestled in the heart of Alleppey, our homestay represents the perfect fusion of Kerala's rich heritage and contemporary luxury. Wake up to misty mornings where water meets sky, surrounded by swaying coconut palms and the symphony of exotic birds.

From traditional Kathakali performances to cooking classes with our family, every moment is designed to immerse you in the rich tapestry of Kerala culture while enjoying modern amenities and personalized hospitality.
